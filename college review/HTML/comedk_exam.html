COMEDK Under Graduate Entrance Test


Overview

COMEDK is the “Consortium of Medical, Engineering and Dental Colleges of Karnataka” to determine the merit of students in an effective, fair, transparent and non-exploitative testing procedure. Presently there are around 190 Engineering colleges which are affiliated to COMEDK and utilize its testing mechanism for taking admission to UG Programme.

Exam Name Consortium of Medical,Engineering and Dental Colleges of karnataka
Exam Category Undergraduate
Exam Level State
Exam Mode Online(Computer based test)

PATTERN

It will be an online computer based test

There will be 3 sections namely Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics

All the questions will be Multiple Choice Questions (MCQ) i.e. each question will have four choices, only one being the correct choice

There will be 60 questions in each section

Candidates will be awarded 1 mark for each correct answer

There will be no negative marking for wrong answers

The medium of the examination will be English Only



NO. of questions in each part is as follows:

Section No. of Questions Marks
Physics 60 60
Chemistry 60 60
Mathematics 60 60
Total 180 180

ELIGIBILITY

All the eligible students in the country can appear for COMEDK examination

SC/ST and OBC candidates belonging to Karnataka state are only eligible to get the benefit of lower eligible qualifying marks

ELIGIBILITY FOR ADMISSION





Qualifying Examination

Second PUC

10+2 Higher Secondary

Equivalent examination recognized by State/Central Govt.

Marks in Qualifying Examination

Candidates need to secure a minimum aggregate of 45% marks (40% marks for SC, ST and OBC candidates of Karnataka State) in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics

Candidates should also have passed these subjects (Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics) individually with English as a compulsory subject

Physics and Mathematics are compulsory subjects along with Chemistry or Bio Technology or Biology or any other technical vocational courses as one of the optional subjects

SYLLABUS

The entrance test is based on the existing syllabus of 10+2/12th STD/2nd PUC
