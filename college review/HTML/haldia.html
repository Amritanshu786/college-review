MODE OF ADMISSION

One needs to take the WBJEE exam or the JEE Main exam.

Admissions for 80% of the seats are done on the basis of rank in WBJEE through WBJEEB Counselling.

Admissions for 10% of the seats are done on the basis of rank in JEE Main through WBJEEB Counselling.

The remaining 10% seats are filled through Management Quota.


AFFILIATION
