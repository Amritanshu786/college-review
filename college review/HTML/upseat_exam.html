Uttar Pradesh State Entrance Examination


OVERVIEW

Uttar Pradesh State Entrance Examination (UPSEE) is conducted by Dr A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University Uttar Pradesh, Lucknow for admission to Government / Government Aided / Private Unaided institutions affiliated to the University or some other state Universities of Uttar Pradesh.

Exam Name Uttar Pradesh State Entrance Examination
Exam Category Undergraduate
Exam Level State
Exam Mode Offline(Pen-Paper)

ABOUT



PAPERS


Courses Papers
B.Tech. / B.Tech. (Agriculture Engineering) Paper 1
B.Tech. (Bio Technology) Paper 1 or Paper 2
B.Des. Paper 1 or Paper 3
B.Pharm. Paper 1 or Paper 2
B.Arch. Paper 3

TYPE


Paper Paper 1
Duration 3 hours
Subjects Physics,Chemistry and Mathematics
Type of Questions 50 Objective type questions in each subject
Total marks 600


Paper Paper 2
Duration 3 hours
Subjects Physics,Chemistry and Biology
Type of Questions 50 Objective type questions in each subject
Total marks 600


Paper Paper 3
Duration 2.5 hours
Subjects Part A: Aesthetic Sensitivity and Mathematics
Part B: Drawing Aptiude
Type of Questions 50 Objective type questions of Mathematics and Aesthetic Sensitivity,2 questions of Drawing Aptitude
Total marks 600

PATTERN

Each objective type question will carry four marks (five marks in Paper 3)

There will be no negative marking

Candidates need to fill the OMR sheet with blue/black ball point pen

All question papers (except Part B Paper-3) will be in Hindi and English both

ELIGIBILITY



Qualifying Examination


Candidates must have passed the intermediate examination of U.P. Board or 10+2 level examination or its equivalent examination

Candidate must have passed without grace the compulsory subjects (Physics & Mathematics) and one optional subject (Chemistry / Bio-Technology / Biology / Technical Vocational subject)

Candidate must have secured an aggregate of 45% (40% for SC/ST candidates) in the above mentioned three subjects

All candidates who are eligible for admission in B.Tech. course are also eligible for B.Tech. (Agriculture Engineering) and B.Tech. (Bio-Technology)
