MODE OF ADMISSION

  • Admission to B.Tech is done on the basis of JEE Main entrance exam.
  • For admission in B.Tech, candidate must have passed 10+2 in science stream with minimum 50% marks. Admission to B.Arch is done on the basis of NATA entrance exam. For B.Arch., candidate must pass 10+2 examination with mathematics as subject and minimum 50% marks
  • Admission to B.Tech is done on the basis of JEE Main entrance exam. For BTech (Lateral), candidate must have passed Diploma or B.Sc. with atleast 45% marks from recognized university. For B.Pharm, candidate must pass Diploma or 10+2 examination with Physics & Chemistry as compulsory subjects.
