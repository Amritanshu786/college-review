Scholarship

100% Scholarship for first year will be provided to students having 93% and above aggregate in class XII.

To continue availing 100% scholarship, students must secure position in top 5% of the merit list for the program.

50% Scholarship for first year will be provided to students having 90% and above aggregate in class XII.

To continue availing 50% scholarship, students must secure position in top 10% of the merit list for the program.
