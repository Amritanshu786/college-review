MODE OF ADMISSION

  • One must have passed the HSC (class 12th) or equivalent examination from a recognized Central or State board with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects and any one of Chemistry, Biology, Biotechnology, or Technical Vocational subject and obtained at least 50% marks (45% for PwD) in the above subjects taken together.
  • One also needs to take the JEE Main exam or Somaiya Entrance Test (SET) or Scholastic Assessment Test (SAT) or MHT-CET exam. Admissions are done on the basis of performance in these examinations through Institutional Counselling.
